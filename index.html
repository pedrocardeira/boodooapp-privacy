BooDoo Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026

BooDoo is designed to help parents and caregivers track baby routines such as milk feeds, sleep, poo, weight, height and daily goals.

BooDoo does not require an account.

The app stores the information you enter locally on your device. This may include baby name, date of birth, feeding records, sleep records, poo records, weight, height, settings and preferences.

BooDoo does not sell your data.

BooDoo does not use advertising.

BooDoo does not provide messaging, social sharing, or user-generated public content.

If you purchase BooDoo Pro, the purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. BooDoo does not receive or store your payment card details.

If you contact support, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. This information is used only to respond to your request.

BooDoo is not a medical diagnosis or treatment app. The information shown in the app is for tracking and routine support only. Always contact a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.
